# Data Retention Policy

The system follows a data minimisation approach.

---

# Stored Data

The system stores:

- candidate company information
- scoring outputs
- compliance findings
- draft messages
- approval decisions
- audit ledger events

---

# Retention Strategy

Retention principles:

- store only data required for system operation
- avoid storing raw scraped content indefinitely
- store derived signals rather than raw inputs where possible

---

# Deletion

Future controls will include:

- scheduled deletion of stale candidates
- removal of unapproved drafts after retention window
- anonymisation of historical analytics data

---

# Compliance Considerations

The design follows privacy-by-design principles.

Relevant frameworks:

- UK ICO data protection guidance
- GDPR data minimisation principles
